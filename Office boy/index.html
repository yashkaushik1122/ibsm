Office Boy

Our office boy service provides comprehensive administrative support and facility management solutions for businesses in Ghaziabad, Uttar Pradesh. From handling administrative tasks to managing the office pantry, our trained professionals ensure the smooth functioning of your workplace, allowing you to focus on your core business activities.


Office Boy act as a helping hand to your office staffs by carrying out daily small routine jobs which otherwise is handled by the employees. Our office boy service, also known as Support Staff Service, in India helps you in saving precious time of your office staff and enable them to perform their task with higher productivity and energy.


Our Office Boy Service Includes:


  • Administrative Support: Our office boys assist with various administrative tasks such as photocopying, filing, and document handling.
  • Office Maintenance: They are responsible for maintaining cleanliness and tidiness in the office premises, including dusting, sweeping, and mopping.
  • Mail Handling: Office boys handle incoming and outgoing mail, ensuring prompt delivery and collection.
  • Refreshment Service: They manage the pantry area, serving tea, coffee, and refreshments to employees and guests.
  • Errand Running: Our office boys can run errands such as purchasing office supplies, banking tasks, and other miscellaneous duties.
